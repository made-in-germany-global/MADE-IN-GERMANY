## 🚀 Vision

> *Connecting premium German manufacturers with global business customers through innovative digital solutions*

**Made in Germany** is an international B2B portal founded by **Andreas Thommen** that serves as the ultimate bridge between German industrial excellence and worldwide business customers. We leverage cutting-edge technology to create seamless connections between manufacturers and bulk buyers worldwide—efficiently, scalably, and digitally.

## 💼 Services

| Service | Description |
|:-------:|:------------|
| 🚢 **Container Orders** | Place orders for complete container shipments directly through our platform |
| 🔄 **Integration** | Seamless onboarding of German manufacturers with XML/CSV product uploads |
| 🚚 **Logistics** | Centralized logistics management via warehouses & shipping partners in Germany |
| 🌐 **Global Network** | Franchise system with local partners in over 50 countries worldwide |
| 💻 **Sales Automation** | Multilingual CRM, lead funnel, and automated sales systems |
| 🔍 **SEO Optimization** | Targeted domains/subdomains for each destination country |
